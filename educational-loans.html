Here are the basic eligibility criteria for Indian Banks to apply for the education loan:
  1. The applicant should be a citizen of India.
  2. The applicant must have attained the age of 18 or else the parents will have to take the loan.
  3. The applicant must have a good academic background.
  4. The applicant must have secured admission to a recognized foreign university/institution/college.
  5. The desired course must be a technical or professional one.
Here is a list of Documents required for applying for an Education Loan:
  1. Filled application form
  2. Photographs
  3. Photo ID.
  4. Residence proof
  5. Academic documents like Mark sheet and certificates of the applicant and Mark sheet (Score Report) of GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS.
  6. Admission proof
  7. Bank statements
  8. Income proof
  9. Bank statements
  10. In case of collateral (immovable property) â€“ Property title deed, Building approved plan, and NOC for a mortgage from builder or society.
The entire loan process is time taking, so it is always advisable to apply for a loan a little early.
Follow these steps for applying:
  1. Check the course first in which you are going to study and check if itâ€™s recognized by the banks or not.
  2. Figure out the total amount of loan you require and how much you are going to arrange on your own.
  3. Compare the student loan provided by different banks so you can select what fits you the most.
  4. Fill out the loan application form, approach the bank later.
  5. After the loan approval, the bank will issue a loan document that includes various elements of the loan.
  6. After this, the bank will disburse the amount in installments or as asked by the university.
Top Education Loans Providers in India:
  1. HDFC Bank

    HDFC Bank is one of the largest banks offering education loans for overseas education, Their process is also faster and more efficient. The maximum loan amount offered by the bank is up to INR 20 lakhs.

  2. State Bank of India (SBI)

    SBI offers a loan of maximum INR 30 lakhs. The Interest rate charged on the loan amount is up to 2%. Further, the loan repayment tenure can be 1 year to 15 years.

  3. Axis Bank

    EAxis bank offers study loans at competitive interest rates. The bank offers a maximum of INR 20 lakhs and covers different costs like tuition, accommodation, and books helping the miscellaneous expenses as well.

  4. Punjab National Bank

    PNB Udaan is the education loan scheme offered by the Punjab National Bank for overseas education. The borrower needs to show a loan margin of 15% for the loan amount of above INR 4 lakhs.
